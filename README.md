INSTRUCTIONS 
CLIENTS AND TOTAL PROJECTS CARDS 
You can go directly to the sidebar on Clients or Projects if you press the cards. 
The graphic shows your income for the last 12 months, if the color is green, it shows 
that it is paid but if it is orange, the payment is pending yet. 
ARCHIVE/EXPORT FUNCTION 
You can export clients, projects, and invoices to excel. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
ARCHIVE/IMPORT FUNCTION 
 
You can import clients, projects, invoices to excel or your database from other 
software. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
CLIENTS TABLE 
 
You can add clients and fill out the information, you can edit or delete the client later. 
At the bottom of the table you can search by Name or Email. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
PROJECTS TABLE 
It’s similar to the clients table but you can see the status of the project. 
CALENDAR 
Here, you will see your project's deadline and the color depends on the status of the 
project. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
INVOICES 
Here you fill in the details and it will be created with the information you provided 
and your company logo. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
INVOICE VIEW 
 
 
 
When you press “View PDF”, the invoice will be downloaded to your computer and 
you will be able to print, send, etc…
